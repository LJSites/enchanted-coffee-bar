✦ Our Story ✦

Coffee, kept curious.

Enchanted opened on South Beach Boulevard with one quiet promise: that a coffee bar could feel like a small adventure. The kind where you walk in for a matcha and walk out with a new favorite snack you couldn't have named twenty minutes earlier.

The menu rotates with what's good. Strawberry shortcake latte in the spring. Avocado truffle toast for the post-hike crowd. Almond croissants while they last. The room? Soft music, warm light, the right amount of bustle.

Come at sunrise. Come at sunset. We're open both ways.
